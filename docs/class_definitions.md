## Class Definitions

1. clean_street
   A street that is mostly clean with minimal or no visible waste.Small natural debris such as leaves or dust is acceptable.

2. construction_waste
   Waste generated from construction activities such as bricks,cement debris,sand piles,or broken building materials.

3. open_waste
   Waste scattered openly on the street or ground without a container.

4. dumpyard
   A location where large amounts of waste are dumped together forming a garbage pile.

5. overfilled_bins
   Bins that have exceeded their capacity with waste spilling outside the container.
